## Social Media Checklist for Students

# Do’s
- Be respectful when you interact with people online, even if you don’t agree with them.
- Be careful about what personal information you share and try to keep your accounts secure.
- Think twice before posting anything, because once it’s online, it’s hard to take back.
- Use social media in a useful way, like learning something new, connecting with others, or staying updated.
- Try to keep a positive and decent online image, especially since it can reflect on you as a student.

 
 # Don’ts
- Don’t share news or information without checking if it’s actually true.
- Avoid posting anything that could be offensive, inappropriate, or hurt someone’s feelings.
- Don’t get involved in unnecessary arguments or negative discussions online.
- Avoid sharing too many personal details that could affect your privacy or safety.
- Don’t spend so much time on social media that it starts affecting your studies or daily routine.
